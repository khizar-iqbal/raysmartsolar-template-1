Every photograph on the site, its source, author, license, and where it's used.

converts each to responsive AVIF/WebP at multiple widths — originals live in
`src/assets/photos/`.

**License rules honoured:**
- *Unsplash License* & *Pexels License*: free for commercial use, no attribution
  required. We credit anyway on `/credits` (good manners).
- *Wikimedia Commons CC BY / CC BY-SA*: **visible attribution required** — these
  images carry an on-page caption credit where they appear, plus `/credits`.
- Photos are used for **atmosphere and comprehension only** and are never captioned
  or implied to be RaySmartSolar's own installations, team, or customers.

## Grading

Every image is displayed through the `.ph` treatment (see `global.css`):
desaturate ~12%, +5% contrast, night-indigo shadow wash + warm highlight cast —
one recipe so the whole set reads as a single commissioned shoot.
